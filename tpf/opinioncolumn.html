Opinion Column

Why Midfielders are the Key to FPL Success

Midfielders often provide the best value for money in FPL. They score goals, provide assists, and earn clean sheet points. Investing in top-performing midfielders can significantly boost your team's performance.

The Importance of Fixture Planning

Planning your transfers around favorable fixtures is crucial. Look ahead at the fixture list and target players from teams with a run of easy games. This strategy can help you maximize points over several gameweeks.

Captaincy Choices: Risk vs. Reward

Choosing the right captain can make or break your gameweek. While it's tempting to always captain the highest-scoring player, sometimes taking a risk on a differential can pay off big time.

To Triple Captain Or Not To

Triple Captaining any player during a game week is often daunting given that it is a chip you only get to play once during the season. You can sit and strategize all you want, but at the end of the day it is simply just an informed risk you take. I believe that when it comes to triple captaining a player you go with your gut feeling.
