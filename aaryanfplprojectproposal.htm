Aaryan's FPL Project Proposal

Objective of the Website

The objective of this project is to design and implement a website dedicated to Fantasy Premier League (FPL) management and analysis. The site will provide users with tools and insights to optimize their FPL teams. Key features will include player performance analytics, fixture schedules, and personalized recommendations based on user-selected criteria. The site aims to help FPL managers make informed decisions and improve their overall performance in the league. Furthermote given that it is something I myself have played for many years with my friends as we are all passionate fans, I'd like to have an opinion column where I can share information from users, friends and other individuals across various social media platofrms all in one page on my website.

Target Audience

The target audience for this website includes Fantasy Premier League players and enthusiasts who are looking for advice, insights, fun fancts, and concise information to enhance their FPL experience. This includes both casual players looking to improve their teams and serious managers seeking in-depth analysis.

Content Plan

The website will consist of the following pages:

  • Home - Overview of the website's features, latest updates, and an introduction to the tools available.
  • Team Deep Dive - Website for viewing information regarding each team based on its current position and previous historical information.
  • Tips & Guides - Articles and guides on FPL strategies, tips for picking players, and common pitfalls to avoid.
  • Contact Us - Contact form and information for user support and feedback.
  • About Us - Information about me, the purpose of this project, my email, social media, and other relevant information.
  • Sitemap - A visual representation of the website's structure to help users navigate the site.
  • Credits - Acknowledgements and credits for contributors and sources used in the project.

Website Structure

The website will be organized as follows:

  • Home
  • Team Deep Dive
  • Tips & Guides
  • Contact Us
  • About Us
  • Sitemap
  • Credits

Each main section will be accessible from the top-level navigation menu, and the site will feature a clear and intuitive layout.
